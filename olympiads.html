Olympiad Excellence Hub

Prepare for IOQM, Regional Mathematical Olympiads (RMO), INMO, and school Olympiads (NSO, IMO, NCO, NSEJS) with advanced study kits and past problems.

🏆
Mathematics
IOQM / RMO / INMO

The Mathematics Olympiad stages led by HBCSE (Tata Institute). Starts with the Indian Olympiad Qualifier (IOQM), leading to RMO, INMO, and the IMO training camp.

Science
NSO / NSEJS / NSEP

National Standard Exams in Junior Science (NSEJS), Physics (NSEP), Chemistry (NSEC), and Biology (NSEB) organized by IAPT, leading to international selection.

SOF Competitions
IMO / NSO / NCO

Science Olympiad Foundation (SOF) school examinations - International Mathematics Olympiad (IMO), National Science Olympiad, and National Cyber Olympiad.
