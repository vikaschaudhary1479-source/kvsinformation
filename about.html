Our mission is to make quality education free and accessible to every student in India — from rural villages to metropolitan cities. No paywalls, no subscriptions, just learning.

Our Mission

Free Education for Every Indian Student

India has 260+ million school students and millions preparing for competitive exams like JEE and NEET. Quality study material has historically been expensive or hard to find, especially for students in smaller towns and villages.

EduVerse India was created to bridge this gap. We believe that a student in a remote village deserves the same quality of study material as one studying in a metro city. All our resources are free, NCERT-aligned, and curated by subject experts.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Educational Resources

Class 1–12 Notes & Solutions

Chapter-wise notes, NCERT solutions, worksheets, and assignments for all classes from 1 to 12, covering all subjects and streams.

JEE & NEET Preparation

Comprehensive JEE Main, JEE Advanced, and NEET UG preparation material including PYQs, mock tests, formula sheets, and revision notes.

Olympiad Resources

Complete preparation material for IOQM, RMO, INMO, IMO, NSO, NCO, NSEP, NSEC, NSEB, and KV Olympiads.

10+ Years PYQs

Previous Year Questions from 2010 to 2025 for CBSE boards, JEE, NEET, and Olympiads — with solutions and answer keys.

Visual Infographics

500+ educational infographics, mind maps, concept diagrams, and formula posters for visual learners across all subjects.

Government Portal Links

Curated links to 30+ official government education portals — CBSE, NCERT, KVS, NTA, DIKSHA, SWAYAM, and more.
