KVS UBI Online Fee Payment

Convenient guide and direct access links for paying Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) school fee via Union Bank of India.

💳
🏦

Union Bank KVS Fee Portal

Official Payment Gateway Partner

Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan collects quarterly tuition fees and VVN online through the Union Bank of India (UBI) portal. You will need your 15-digit Student Unique ID and Date of Birth to log in.

Important Notice: Fee collection is open from the 1st to 15th of the starting month of each quarter. Ensure payment is completed on time to avoid late fines.
Go to Online Fee Portal Mobile Pay Portal
How to Pay Online
1
Access the UBI Portal

Click the green portal button above to open the Union Bank of India KVS Fee Collection login interface.

2
Enter Student Credentials

Input the 15-digit Student Unique ID (provided by school office) and student's Date of Birth (DD/MM/YYYY format) along with the captcha code.

3
Verify details & Pay

Review the student details, class, school fee due amount, and click 'Make Payment' using Net Banking, Debit/Credit Card, or UPI.

4
Print Payment Receipt

Immediately download and print the receipt generated by UBI. Keep a soft copy and submit a hard copy to your class teacher.
